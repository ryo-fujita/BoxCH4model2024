The global sectorial anthropogenic CH4 emissions for 1970-2015 (yearly) were adopted from Emissions Database for Global Atmospheric Research version 6.0 (EDGARv6) (Crippa et al., 2021). The historical emissions for 1890–1960 (decadal) were taken from EDGAR-HYDE v1.4 (Olivier & Berdowski, 2001; van Aardenne et al., 2001). The a priori anthropogenic CH4 emissions in 1750 were specified from Lassey et al. (2007a) for each source category and the CH4 emissions for EDGAR in 1850 were specified by CEDS (Hoesly et al. 2018).
